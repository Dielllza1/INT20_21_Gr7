Little Story

Anton's is a nostalgic New York café and wine bar in the heart of the West Village. The vision of Chef Nick Anderer and General Manager/Beverage Director Natalie Johnson, Anton's thoughtfully reimagines NYC's rich culinary history through comforting, eclectic American cuisine with deep European influences.
